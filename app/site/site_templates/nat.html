What is a NAT?

A NAT (Non-Arbitrary Token) is a framework/application of Digital Matter Theory (DMT) that leverages the infrastructure of Bitcoin to generate unique digital assets/ownership. NAT's uses the data patterns of Bitcoin to create tokens which then can be turned into art/games etc.

Digital Matter Theory (DMT)

DMT presents the creation of digital substance from patterns in data. One example of DMT is bitmap. Bitmap identifies every block from bitcoin to generate digital land in the metaverse.

Why this card?

This card provides digital ownership that signifies you are a student of Coding Temple. There will be a total of 20,000 cards, each costing $200 or 0.003 BTC. When students graduate, they can sell their membership card to onboard new students.

Deployment

Deployment takes place on Mscribe, the marketplace founded by the creators of NAT's. Deployment guild is in the gitbook.

Visit the Mscribe website to explore the marketplace, discover existing NATs.
