Hybrid

The integration of blockchain into education is inevitable. As an early adopter, our school can be a leader in shaping the future of learning. We propose a hybrid approach that combines the best of web2 and web3 experiences, allowing us to explore the blockchain and metaverse technologies while maintaining our traditional strengths. This positions us to create more access, agency, and opportunity for our students in a blockchain-powered world.

Expansion Ideas

Benefits

  • Reduced cost for students, making education more accessible and affordable
  • Increased revenue for Coding Temple
  • More connection to students and staff, fostering a community
  • Exposure to cutting-edge blockchain technology, preparing students for the future of digital assets and ownership
  • Increased brand awareness and recognition for Coding Temple as an innovative educational institution
  • Partnerships and collaborations with blockchain-based projects and companies
  • Increased student engagement and motivation through the ownership and trading of unique digital assets
  • Opportunities for students to learn about digital asset management, trading, and investing as part of their education
